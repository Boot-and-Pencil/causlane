## Limitation note

M09.4 exposes explicit overload modes for the in-process adapter only:
`Wait` and `FailFast`. `PartitionKey` remains the host-supplied
tenant/backpressure bucket boundary. Priority scheduling, automatic tenant
derivation, background shedding workers, distributed queue storage, `RateLimit`
enforcement, retry policy and numeric operational SLO threshold enforcement
remain outside M09.4.
