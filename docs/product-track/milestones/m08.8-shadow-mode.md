## Notes

The delivered M08.8 surface is intentionally small: host integrations collect
runtime events through `InProcessRuntime::subscribe()` and compare them with
`ShadowExpectation` using `compare_shadow_events`. Mismatches are returned as a
diagnostic `ShadowComparison`; they do not feed back into runtime behavior.

Full migration/shadow adoption guidance remains tracked by M12.3.
