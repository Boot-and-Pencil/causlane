# ADR-0031: AI-Assisted Development Provenance

## Status

Accepted.

## Context

Causlane development may use AI assistance. Public release requires clear
authorship and provenance expectations.

## Decision

AI assistance is allowed. AI tools are not authors, co-authors, maintainers,
reviewers or signers.

Human contributors remain responsible for code, documentation, security and
license compliance.

Material AI assistance may be disclosed with `Assisted-by:` trailers or pull
request notes. Do not use `Co-authored-by:` for AI tools.

## Consequences

- Commit authorship remains human or organization-controlled.
- AI usage can be transparent without confusing legal authorship.
- Contributors have explicit review obligations.
